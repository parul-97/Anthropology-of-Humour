#### Resources
* **Jupyter Notebook shortcuts**
   * Run a cell of code: Shift+Enter
   * Insert a cell above: Esc-A
   * Insert a cell below: Esc-B
  
* **Web scraping**
   * Inspect Element in Chrome: Command+Shift+C
